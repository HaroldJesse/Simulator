AMALTHEA

Is the third closest moon to Jupiter. It is part of the Inner group with an irregular diameter of 250 km by 146 km by 128 km and a mass of 208 times 10 to the 16th power kilograms. It has an orbit with a semimajor axis of 181366 km, and an Eccentricity of 0.0075 Its inclination is 2.565 degrees and the orbital period is 12 hours 01 minutes 46seconds. The absolute magnitude is 7.1. Amalthea was discovered in 1892 by Barnard.
