IO

Is the fifth closest moon to Jupiter. It is part of the Galilean group with a diameter of 3660.0 km by 3637.4 km by 3630.0 km and a mass of 8931900 kilograms times 10 to the 16th power. It has an orbit with a semimajor axis of 421700 km, and an Eccentricity of 0.0041 Its inclination is 0.050 degrees and the orbital period is 1.7691 days. The absolute magnitude is -1.7. Io was discovered in 1610 by Galileo Galilei and Simon Marius.
