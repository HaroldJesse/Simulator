METIS

Is the closest moon to Jupiter. It is part of the Inner group with an irregular diameter of 60 km by 40 km by 34 km and a mass of 3.6 kilograms times 10 to the 16th power. It has an orbit with a semimajor axis of 128852 km, and an Eccentricity of 0.0077 Its inclination is 2.226 degrees and the orbital period is 7 hours 10 minutes 16 seconds. The absolute magnitude is 10.5. Metis was discovered in 1979 by Synnott through Voyager1.
