THEBE

Is the fourth closest moon to Jupiter. It is part of the Inner group with an irregular diameter of 116 km by 98 km by 84 km and a mass of ~43 times 10 to the 16th power kilograms. It has an orbit with a semimajor axis of 222452 km, and an Eccentricity of 0.0180 Its inclination is 2.909 degrees and the orbital period is 16 hours 16 minutes 02seconds. The absolute magnitude is 9.0. Thebe was discovered in 1979 by Synnott through Voyager 1.
